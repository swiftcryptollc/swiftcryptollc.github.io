Our Cardano [ADA for Warriors](https://4wardpool.swiftcryptollc.com){:target="_blank"} pool (4WARD) donates 50% of the pool operator rewards to the [Third Option Foundation](https://www.thirdoptionfoundation.org/){:target="_blank"} during the last epoch of each month our pool was active.  The Third Option Foundation offers assistance to fallen and injured CIA paramilitary operators and their families. The assistance can be in the form of financial and logistical support, academic and college prep for children, home maintenance, counseling and family services, physical health and wellness support, legacy activities to honor fallen warriors, and the establishment of memorials.
