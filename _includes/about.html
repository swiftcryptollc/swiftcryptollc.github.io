About Swift Crypto LLC

Swift Crypto LLC is a small crypto mining business based in Leesburg VA USA. It was started in 2021 to mine Ethereum. Since that time, it has expanded to mining other crypto-currencies and operating a Cardano pool.

For additional info on the Swift Crypto LLC Cardano 4WARD pool see the Third Option Foundation announcement.


Steven Fisher, the owner and operator of Swift Crypto LLC, brings over 22 years of Software and Systems Engineering experience.
