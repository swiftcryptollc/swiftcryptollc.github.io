About Swift Crypto LLC

Swift Crypto LLC is a small crypto mining business based in Leesburg VA. It was started in 2021 to mine Ethereum. Since that time, it has expanded to mining other crypto-currencies and operating a Cardano pool.

The Swift Crypto LLC Cardano 4WARD pool will be donating a portion of it's income to a charity. The details are still being worked out, but I'm hoping to have an update soon!


Steven Fisher, the owner and operator of Swift Crypto LLC, brings over 22 years of Software and Systems Engineering experience.
