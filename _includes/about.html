About Swift Crypto LLC

Swift Crypto LLC is a small business crypto mining company based in Leesburg VA USA. It was started in 2021 to mine Ethereum. Since that time, it has expanded to operating the ADA for Warriors (4WARD) Cardano pool.

Our Cardano pool donates 30% of our operator rewards to the Third Option Foundation, 10% to 500 Rising, and another 10% to Captive Audience. See our ADA for Warriors website for further information.


Steven Fisher, the owner and operator of Swift Crypto LLC, brings over 22 years of Software and Systems Engineering experience.
