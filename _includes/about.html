About Swift Crypto LLC

Swift Crypto LLC is a small crypto mining business based in Leesburg VA. It was started in 2021 to mine Ethereum. Since that time, it has expanded to mining other crypto-currencies and operating a Cardano validator under the ticker "4WARD".

Steven Fisher, the owner and operator of Swift Crypto LLC, brings over 22 years of Software and Systems Engineering experience.
